Who I Am

Travel Vlogger & Medical Student

I'm Mirembe, a Travel Vlogger and Medical Student from Angola studying in Bulgaria

On my channel, you'll find vlogs documenting my travels across Europe during school breaks. I visit new countries and cities to experience new cultures and cuisines. Expect videos showcasing the top sights, hidden gems, restaurants, cafes and nightlife in each destination.

You'll also get an inside look at medical school through study vlogs. I share how I manage my courseload, take exams, handle stress and practical tips for succeeding in med school. My goal is to inspire and help other aspiring doctors around the world.

In addition to travel and study vlogs, I post videos about topics like adapting to life abroad, making international friends, learning new languages and more.

Overall, my channel is about crossing borders, discovering the world and following my dreams. Subscribe and join me on these amazing adventures!
